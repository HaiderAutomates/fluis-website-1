How AI Costs Work

What You Pay Us

A flat monthly fee for the website, agent, CRM, and automations. No hidden charges. No per-conversation fees from us.

AI Usage (Pass-Through)

AI providers charge per conversation. Voice calls cost roughly 30–80p per 5-minute call (under $1). We pass this through at cost — zero markup.

Most businesses spend less than $30/month on AI usage.

Pricing Questions

Is there a contract?

Never. Month-to-month billing. Cancel anytime — no cancellation fees, no lock-in, no awkward phone calls. We earn your business every single month.

What’s included in the setup fee?

Everything. Your website, CRM account, AI agent configuration, business knowledge training, automation workflows, and calendar setup. We build it all — you just tell us about your business.

What if the agent doesn’t book any appointments?

You don’t pay. Our entire model is Results as a Service. If the AI doesn’t book a single appointment in any given month, that month is free. During the 7-day trial, if you’re not happy — full refund including the setup fee.

How long does setup take?

3 days for onboarding (we build everything), then a 7-day free trial where the agent goes live. That’s 10 days from sign-up to your first billing — and only if you’re happy.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Yes, anytime. Moving from Website to Chat Agent or Chat to Voice is seamless — we handle the migration. Downgrading works the same way, effective next billing cycle.

What about my existing website?

We can embed our AI agents on your existing website instead of building a new one. Same pricing, same features. We just add the chat or voice widget to your current site.
